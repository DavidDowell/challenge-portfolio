About Me

Hi, I am David Dowell. I am a 26 year old man, who recently ditched his comfortable career in retail food sales to pursue web development and software engineering. My former job did not challenge me the way I hoped it would and I started thinking of easy solutions to improve the job I was doing if only I knew how to build an application or website. Working different jobs has given me perspective on how important technology is and how this field is growing at a rapid pace.

I challenged myself by taking the UNC coding bootcamp to learn the abilities I was searching for with the help of professionals currently in the web development and software engineering field. Here I learned the fundamentals of web development, how to engineer a full stack web application, and how to optimize web applications.
