About Me

Hi, I am David Dowell. I am a 26 year old man, who recently ditched his comfortable career in retail food sales to pursue web development and software engineering. I previously had a background in science, receiving a bachelor's degree in Biological Sciences at the University of Southern Mississippi, but I did not pursue a career in this field. I fell out of love with Biology, but I maintained love for science and gained a new appreciation for technology and the engineers who give this technology life. Thats why I decided I wanted to be one of these people to bring tech to life through code.

I challenged myself by taking the UNC coding bootcamp to learn the abilities I was searching for with the help of professionals currently in the web development and software engineering field. Here I learned the fundamentals of web development, how to engineer a full stack web application, and how to optimize web applications.
